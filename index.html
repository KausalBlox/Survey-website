Sustainability Survey

CQU Social Innovation Project

Help Shape a Sustainable Future

Climate change and environmental degradation are among the most pressing challenges of our time. As we face rising temperatures, increasing pollution, and diminishing natural resources, understanding community perspectives on sustainability practices becomes crucial for developing effective solutions.

This survey aims to gather insights from the Australian community about current environmental awareness, sustainable practices, and barriers to eco-friendly living. Your responses will help CQU researchers identify trends, challenges, and opportunities in promoting sustainable lifestyles across different demographics and regions.

The survey takes approximately 5-7 minutes to complete and covers topics including daily environmental practices, energy usage, transportation choices, and future sustainability goals. Your participation will contribute to valuable research that may influence policy recommendations and community education programs focused on environmental protection and sustainable development.

All responses are anonymous and will be used solely for research purposes. Thank you for taking the time to share your perspectives and help us work towards a more sustainable future for Australia.

Survey Details:

  • Duration: 5-7 minutes
  • Questions: 15 topic-related questions
  • Anonymous and confidential
  • Mobile-friendly design
